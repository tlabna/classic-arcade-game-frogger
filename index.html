Arcade Game

Using your arrow keys help the Princess get to the other side as many times as you can in 1 minute. You get +1 point if you get to the other side safely.

Collect the following items for extra bonuses:

  • Bug speed decreases
  • +5 points
  • +1 life

Avoid the following items:

  • -1 life
  • -1 life
  • -1 life
